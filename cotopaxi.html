Cotopaxi

Cotopaxi is one of the most renowned summits in Ecuador. It is part of the Andes mountains and is located in the city of Latacunga in the province Cotopaxi from which it gets its name. Its close proximity to the capital, Quito, makes it a popular attraction. Standing at 5.847 meters it is also one of the highest active volcanoes in the world. Cotopaxi is considered an active stratovolcano. Stratovolcanoes are known to have steep sides and a conical shape. They are also more likely to produce explosive eruptions. If you are visiting Ecuador, a trip to Cotopaxi is a must, especially for avid mountain climbers.
